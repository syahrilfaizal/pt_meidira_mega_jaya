PT Meidira Megah Jaya, is a dynamic and innovative company based in Balikpapan, East Kalimantan, offering comprehensive one-stop solutions for construction, installation, trade, and technology services, driven by a commitment to quality, customer satisfaction, and industry-leading expertise.

About Us

Since our inception on July 30, 2021, we have been a dynamic and forwardthinking company committed to providing one-stop solution for a wide range of construction and trade services. With a diverse portfolio spanning construction, installation, trade, and technology, we are dedicated to transforming ideas into reality. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction sets us apart as a leader in the industry.

Our Vision

To be the best General Supplier company in Indonesia

that emphasizes continuous development, satisfied customers, and the welfare of stakeholders through the implementation of (good corporate governance) and commitment to social responsibility (corporate social responsibility).

Our Mission

Uniform views between management and employees in maintaining company value and achieving goals together.

Maximizing the implementation of risk management, marketing, operations, finance, human resources, technology, and information effective and efficient.

The application of the precautionary principle in every decision making and good corporate governance.

Provide training and carry out the recruitment process of resources the right human resources to produce a competent workforce patent in their field, dedicated and passionate about culture companies to achieve common goals.

Increasing competition through collaboration with various parties in theconstruction sector on a national and inter national scale.
